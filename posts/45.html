Assuming you have the time to read through this book, you are likely better off searching the web or calling an advisor to answer your questions.

If this book were 5-10$, then I might be inclined to recommend it as a high-level overview of investment options available to Canadians, but it only covers each topic to a point where you know what it is and a little bit about it. There is no depth to the book, and it costs around 20$ after tax, so I do not recommend buying it.

As a side note, the Canadian Couch Potato site is a useful reference for those who want simple, to the point, investment advice for the average Canadian.
